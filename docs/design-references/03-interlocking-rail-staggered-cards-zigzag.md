## Structural patterns to borrow

- **Dark / ink “rail” band:** Full-width band carrying **left-weighted headline + CTA** while the right side hosts **vertical cards** that **bleed below** the band into the next light section — classic **interlock** (no hard horizon).
- **Staggered feature cards:** Uniform card height for rhythm, but **entry** via overlap across section boundary.
- **Zig-zag features:** Alternate text-left/image-right vs reverse; **satellite** pills or small panels breaking the outer box silhouette.
- **Testimonial / proof row:** Three-up grid with **one** elevated focal card (contrast via ink panel or border — accent still within 5% rule).
- **List vs float:** Later section pairs **dense list rhythm** with calm typographic column for contrast.
