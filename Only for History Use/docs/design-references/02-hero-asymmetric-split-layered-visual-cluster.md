## Structural patterns to borrow

- **Asymmetric split (~45/55 or similar):** Text column ≠ media column width; **heavy type + CTA** on one side balances **complex layered cluster** on the other (optical balance without mirrored boxes).
- **Layered z-stack (right/visual column):** Background watermark or soft geometry → **primary panel** → **smaller satellite panel** overlapping a corner (staggered silhouette). Use shadows/blur sparingly; prefer **clip, border, and ink** per directive.
- **Section interlock:** Solid or ink **horizontal band** at hero foot with **next section’s cards** overlapping upward — breaks the “stacked rectangles” baseline (negative margin / absolute overlap).
- **Eyebrow → headline → body → CTA** vertical rhythm on the text side; CTA is the bottom anchor.
